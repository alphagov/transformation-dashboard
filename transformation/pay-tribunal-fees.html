Benefits for users

If you’re taking part in an employment tribunal you will be able to pay your fees using an online service

Scope of exemplar

Civil claims are a mass market way for the public to solve legal disputes or obtain money owed. The current 2 services are Money Claims Online (MCOL) and Possession Claims Online (PCOL). The exemplar aims to create a digital service for all types of claim, improve user experience and save money.
