Digital strategy statement

E-passport gates are a secure and convenient self-service alternative to the conventional border control process. There are ambitious plans in place for expansion of and improvements to the e-passport gate.s

From the 'Home Office digital strategy'' – December 2012

Benefits for users

If you’re eligible to join the scheme, from April 2014 you’ll be able to use e-Passport gates at the border (where available) — making it faster and easier to enter the UK.

Scope of exemplar

Phase 2 (due April 2014) will allow eligible travellers to use the e-Passport gates. Travellers will need to have a compatible biometric ‘chipped’ passport to be able to use this service.
