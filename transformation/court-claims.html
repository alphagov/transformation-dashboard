Scope of exemplar

Civil claims are a way for the public to solve civil legal disputes or obtain money or property owed. The exemplar will create a digital service initially for accelerated possession claims, to improve the user experience and the efficiency of the service.

For further information about this project please visit our blog or contact us.

Digital inclusion

GDS has mapped the exemplars against the digital inclusion scale to help show where these services may be difficult for some people to use. See the rating for Civil claims.
