Digital strategy statement

…a single intelligent interface which adapts the questions it asks the user based on business rules: avoiding unnecessary questions and providing a dynamic, streamlined process that users (many of whom are not native English speakers) find simple and accessible.

From the Home Office digital strategy – December 2012

Benefits for users

If you need a visa to visit the UK you will be able to apply using a simple online service

Business case

Better visa process is a political priority to drive UK growth by promoting visits from tourists, students and business people. Currently 3.4m visas are issued at a cost £767m/year, £223 per transaction.

Visa liberalisation could deliver up to £2.8bn / year from extra tourism : a simpler, clearer, faster digital process offers a similar opportunity.

  • Admin saving - unknown and highly dependent on backend involvement
  • Economic boost: visa liberalisation could deliver up to £2.8bn / year from extra tourism - a more straightforward digital process is a similar type of opportunity
