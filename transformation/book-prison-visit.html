Digital strategy statement

…our single biggest transactional service, which at the moment is a phone- or email-based, manual data entry system.

From the Ministry of Justice digital strategy – December 2012

Benefits for users

If you want to visit someone in prison you will be able to book using an online service without waiting to speak to prison staff

Business case

Different prisons book visits in different ways. It is mostly done by phone or email and often requires family members to spend a long time getting through on the phone. A digital channel will cut administrative costs to the National Offender Management Service (NOMS) and make the process easier for families, encouraging more to visit.

  • Up to £2m saving from staff reduction in the medium term
  • Easier to change visit times, hence more visits and better opportunity for rehabilitation
  • Redeploy prison service staff to higher value non-administrative activity
