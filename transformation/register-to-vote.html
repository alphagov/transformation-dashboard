Scope of exemplar

ERTP will transform the electoral registration process by introducing Individual Electoral Registration (IER). Instead of one person in a household supplying the details of all people living at the same address (which can result in fraud and errors), IER will require people to register individually.

To support IER and make it simpler for users, a new digital channel will be created and a method of confirming identities will be introduced. IER is intended to rebuild trust and modernise our electoral system.

For more information about this project please contact us via our feedback form.
