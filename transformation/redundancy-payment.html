Scope of exemplar

When an employer becomes insolvent employees are often owed money (especially for redundancy pay). The Redundancy Payments Service processes claims for statutory redundancy payments, which are paid from the National Insurance Fund (NIF). The present system is entirely paper based - this leads to unnecessary cost as well as delays. We are not able to predict benefits accurately at this discovery stage, but the current transactional spend makes us confidently expect improved customer service and significant annual benefits, leading to a substantial (though currently undetermined) positive ROI.
