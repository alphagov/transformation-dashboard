Scope of exemplar

Personal Independence Payment was introduced in April 2013 for people aged 16 to 64 to help with the extra costs of long-term ill-health or disability. Users can make a claim over the telephone or through the post and may need to attend a face-to-face consultation with a health professional to assess how much support they need.

We’re now building an in-depth understanding of user needs to see which parts of the PIP process might best be delivered through digital channels.

For more information about this project please contact us via our feedback form.
